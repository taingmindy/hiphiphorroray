What is Hip Hip Horror-ay?

Hip Hip Horror-ay was created to bring together a community of horror film fanatics, scaredy cats, and everyone inbetween. Horror films have a way of gripping us at the edge of our seats, forcing us to step outside of our comfort zones and immerse ourselves in a state of uncertainty.

Whether you're here to find the perfect horror movie for date night, or to try something new, all are welcome here.
